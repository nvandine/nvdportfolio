about me

Who am I? I am a web developer, a connector, a researcher. I am not: a tylenol, a chair, a water bottle.

I'm looking to find a position as a web developer, while maintaining my educational and program development background to mentor future coders of all ages, experience and background. I'm really into accessibility.

I have a preternatural amount of energy, which I expend in excellent ways: I love long-distance cycling and cross-country skiing, supporting and occasionally participating in Toronto's arts, theatre and comedy scene. I have built three spaceships (for films; they don't work.... yet). If I am sitting down I'm probably reading.

Have you ever wondered if Kool-Aid Man — a giant, sentient glass jug — would actually be able to bust through a brick wall? If you take a standard jug and scale it to approximately 6 feet tall, the thickness of the jug would be around 3.6 inches, practically unbreakable. He would, however, crack. And if the Kool-Aid inside is his lifeblood, he would lose just enough from the impact to be pretty tired. So yeah, he could break through the wall, but he wouldn't have the energy to say "Oh Yeah!".

See? Meticulous attention to detail.
